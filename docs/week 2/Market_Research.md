## 4. Market Analysis  

### Key Observations:
- Most platforms focus on **staff scheduling and compliance**  
- Limited focus on **user experience for participants**  
- High cost and complexity in existing solutions  
- Lack of innovation in **AI-based support systems**  

---

## 5. Gaps in the Market  

The following problems remain unsolved:

- No AI chatbot support for NDIS-related queries  
- Limited self-service options for participants  
- Complex interfaces not suitable for all users  
- High dependency on admin intervention  

---

## 6. Opportunity for Proposed System  

The Adelaide Care Connect system can improve by:
- Introducing an **AI-powered chatbot**  
- Providing a **simple and accessible user interface**  
- Enabling **self-service booking and management**  
- Offering a **cost-effective and scalable solution**  

---

## 7. Summary  

While existing systems provide strong operational tools, there is a clear opportunity to improve **user experience, accessibility, and intelligent automation**, particularly within the Australian NDIS context.
